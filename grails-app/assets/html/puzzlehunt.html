I built this website after we ran Bloomberg's first puzzlehunt and realized we had a logistics issue. The people giving out the puzzle materials needed a quick way to see who had access to which puzzles and the thirty people in needed an efficient way to be assigned hint requests and communicates with the teams. I built this site with logins for players, admins, hinters and the copy room, with the ability for eveyone to communicate.
One of the coolest properties is that all of the puzzles, players, rewards and even style of the website are defined by a single json config file. Nothing is hard-coded; it's all ready for next year.

- java, groovy, grails
